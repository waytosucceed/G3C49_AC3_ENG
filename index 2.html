Two types of contractions:


a) Pronoun + Verb Contractions
Examples: I'm (I am), You've (You have), She'd (She had), We'll (We will)

Pronoun Verb Contraction
He is He’s
She is She’s
It is It’s
We are We’re
You are You’re
They are They’re

b) Contractions with "not"
Examples: wasn't (was not), don't (do not), won't (will not)

Verb Not Contraction
Is not Isn’t
Are not Aren’t
Were not Weren’t
Have not Haven’t
Has not Hasn’t
Does not Doesn’t
Can not Can’t
